# Lab 02: Zero Trust Policy Profile

## 1. ZTA Component Definitions

- **Policy Engine (PE)** : The "Brain" or the decision maker of the system. It uses algorithms to evaluate multiple risk signals against the organization's security protocol
- **Policy Administrator (PA)**: The "Rule Setter". It does not make the decision itself but takes the decision from the Policy Engine and translates it into action
- **Policy Enforcement Point (PEP)**: The "Gatekeeper". It has no decision making power and only follows orders from the PA.
